# Advanced Exercise 03: Evaluate An Async Boundary

## Target Skill

Make a senior-level decision about whether async belongs in this app.

## Change Request

- Identify a realistic future requirement that might pressure the app toward async work.
- Design both a synchronous and async-friendly approach.
- Argue which one should be chosen now.

## What The Coach Should Press On

- Is the future requirement believable?
- What does async buy here that sync does not?
- What complexity cost would async impose on the codebase?

## Success Criteria

- the reasoning is anchored to this app
- the tradeoff analysis is concrete
- async is chosen or rejected on substance, not style
